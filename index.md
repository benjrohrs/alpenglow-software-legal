# Privacy Policy — Cap Counter

**Effective date:** May 7, 2026

Cap Counter ("the app") is a water polo coaching tool published by Alpenglow Software ("we", "us"). This policy describes what data the app handles and how.

## What data the app collects

**None.** Cap Counter does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information about you, your players, or your teams. The app does not include analytics, advertising, tracking, or third-party SDKs.

## Data stored on your device

The app stores everything you enter — players, rosters, games, events, settings — locally on your iPad using Apple's Core Data framework. This data:

- Stays on your device
- Is never sent to us, Apple, or any third party
- Is removed automatically if you delete the app

You can clear all stored data at any time from the app's Settings screen.

## Network access

The app does not make any network requests. It works fully offline.

## Children's privacy

Cap Counter is intended for adult coaches and their use is the only data input. We do not knowingly collect any data from children. Player names, cap numbers, and game information entered by a coach remain on the coach's device and are never transmitted to us.

## Future changes

If we ever change the app to collect data — for example, to enable cloud sync between devices — this policy will be updated and users will be notified before the change takes effect.
